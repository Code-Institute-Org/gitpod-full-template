About us

My name is Jess Smyth. I founded Skill Share in 2017 after struggling to find work. I had taken time out to raise a family. When my daughter started pre-school I started looking for work. I stumbled across a temporary job, part-time. After this, I didn't have another job for a full year. I didn't even get called for interviews. Why was this so hard? I finally found a job through a family friend. I wondered how many others were in a similar position, but didn't have someone to make an introduction
